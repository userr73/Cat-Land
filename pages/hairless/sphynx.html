Health

Grooming

High - weekly baths required to prevent skin irritation and oil buildup. Weekly ear cleaning is also essential to avoid wax buildup.

Health needs/concerns

Sphynx cats have a high metabolism to maintain body heat, sometimes requiring more food than typical cat breeds. Some healthy concerns to watch out for include Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (HCM) and environmental sensitivity.

Lifespan

8 to 15 years
