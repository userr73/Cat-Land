Health

Grooming

Very high - requires daily brushing with a metal comb to prevent matting and baths every 2-6 weeks to manage oily skin.

Health needs/concerns

Their flat faces cause narrow nostrils and malformed airways, leading to difficulty breathing and heat intolerance. Other health concerns include constant eye discharge, dental overcrowding, Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD) and Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (HCM).
