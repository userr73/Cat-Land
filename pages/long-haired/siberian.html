Health

Grooming

Moderate to high - require brushing 2 to 3 times a week to prevent matting. Daily attention is needed during heavy seasonal shedding in spring and autumn.

Health needs/concerns

Genetically predisposed to Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (HCM) and Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD), and are also prone to dental disease if their teeth are not brushed.

Lifespan

12 to 18 years
