Physical Characteristics

Coat Length

Long and dense (5 to 10cm)

Coat Colour and/or Pattern

Colourpoint pattern (light-coloured body, usually white to cream, with darker 'points' on the face, ears, legs, and tail). Common point colours include seal, blue, red, lilac, and chocolate. Other patterns could include tabby or tortoiseshell patterns.

Fur texture

Fine, silky, and glossy

Weight

Females: 3.2 to 5kg

Males: 4.5 to 6.3kg

Height

25 to 30cm

Health

Grooming

Very high - they require daily brushing to prevent severe matting and tangles. They also require daily eye wiping to prevent tear staining.

Health needs/concerns

These cats are prone to eye discharge, dental crowding and progressive retinal atrophy. Other health concerns include Brachycephalic Airway Syndrome and Polycystic Kidney Disease (PKD).

Lifespan

9 to 15 years (some live up to 18 years with great care)
