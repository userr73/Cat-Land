Physical Characteristics

Coat Length

Available in both shorthair and longhair varieties

Coat Colour and/or Pattern

All colours and patterns are accepted, excluding colourpoint patterning, which is generally not permitted.

Fur texture

Shorthaired cats have a hard, glossy outer coat with a cottony undercoat, whereas longhaired varieties have a soft, silky, and well-padded texture.

Weight

Females: 3.2 to 5kg

Males: 4.5 to 5.9kg

Height
