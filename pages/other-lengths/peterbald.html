Health

Grooming

High, especially for hairless and flock varieties, requiring weekly baths to prevent oil buildup. Their large ears and eyes also need to be cleaned regularly to prevent wax and dirt buildup.

Health needs/concerns

Environmental sensitivity, feline ectodermal dysplasia, Progressive Retinal Atrophy (PRA) and hypertrophic cardiomyopathy.

Lifespan

10 to 15 years
