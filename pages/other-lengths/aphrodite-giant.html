Physical Characteristics

Coat Length

Short-haired or semi-longhaired

Coat Colour and/or Pattern

All colours and patterns except for cinnamon, chocolate, mink, fawn, lilac and pointed. Solid and bicolour patterns are common.

Fur texture

Soft and silky

Weight

Large and heavy boned - typically weighs between 5 to 11kg.

Height

Around 33 to 36cm on average

Health

Grooming

At least 2-3 times a week, especially for long-haired varieties.

Health needs/concerns

Obesity, joint and heart issues, ear infections and hairballs. A balanced, protein-rich diet is required to prevent weight gain.

Lifespan

12 to 15 years
